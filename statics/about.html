  1. The Association Camerounaise pour le Marketing Social (ACMS) was created in 1996 and is registered under receipt N° 21/RD/LOI/BAPP of August 30, 1996. Its head office is based in Yaoundé, with regional branches in Garoua, Maroua, Ngaoundéré, Bertoua and Douala. It is a non-profit organization whose aim is to improve the living conditions of vulnerable populations.
  2. ACMS has over 25 years' experience in implementing high-impact interventions and strengthening healthcare systems in the public, private and community sectors. This expertise extends to HIV/AIDS prevention, sexual and reproductive health, malaria control, child survival, public health event management, governance, research, monitoring and evaluation, and innovative solutions. ACMS also distributes:
    • Prudence Plus Prestige male condoms
    • Female condoms,
    • Oral contraceptives,
    • Injectable contraceptives,
    • Long-acting impregnated mosquito nets,
    • Home water treatment solution.

We take immediate action wherever the need arises through innovative solutions to help communities build their well-being.

  • Intégrité: Nous sommes responsables et redevables envers nos bénéficiaires et nos partenaires. Nous devons agir en tout lieu et dans toutes les circonstances possibles dans la transparence et l’honnêteté.
  • Respect: Nous accordons du prix aux bénéficiaires de nos interventions et nous ne portons pas porter des jugements de valeur sur leurs modes de vie et de pensée. Nous savons qu’ensemble et unis, nous pouvons résoudre les problèmes les plus complexes.
  • Egalité: Nous croyons à l'importance de respecter et d'honorer chaque individu ; Nous accordons les mêmes valeurs et considérations aux partenaires et bénéficiaires y compris toute l’équipe de notre organisation tout en agissant en toute transparence sans aucun jugement fondé sur le sexe, la race, l’ethnie, la religion, et le handicap.
  • Efficacité: Nous intervenons avec professionnalisme et un grand esprit de redevabilité constante envers nos cibles de sorte à toujours atteindre les impacts recherchés dans le cadre de nos interventions

  • Integrity: We are responsible and accountable to our beneficiaries and partners. We must act wherever and whenever possible with transparency and honesty.
  • Respect: We value the beneficiaries of our interventions, and we do not pass value judgments on their way of life or way of thinking. We know that, together and united, we can solve the most complex problems.
  • Equality: We believe in the importance of respecting and honoring each individual; we accord the same values and considerations to partners and beneficiaries, including all our organization's staff, while acting transparently without any judgment based on gender, race, ethnicity, religion or disability.
  • Effectiveness: We intervene with professionalism and a strong spirit of constant accountability towards our targets, so as to always achieve the desired impacts of our interventions
